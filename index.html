Animal Rescue Action Network

ARAN (Animal Rescue Action Network) is a non-profit orginization founded to save the lives of impounded dogs

Boris

NEEDS ADOPTION: Boris is a young Labrador x Huntaway that is 10 months old. He is energetic, affectionate and a people dog. He is good around kids and fine around dogs. ARAN has desexed, vaccinated, microchipped and registered so there wil be an adoption cost of $300

Nel

Nell is a super special girl, she has a lovely nature, gets on with everyone and is extremely smart. Within a day of leaving the pound she had learned her new name and was coming when called. Nell is 12 months od

Bhakti

Bhakti, ( a name which means devotion) is a gorgeous 18 month old mastiff x (medium sized) girl. She has so much love to give and wants to do nothing but play and cuddle, cuddle and play. She has heaps of energy and there is nothing she would like more that a doggy buddy to play with.

These dogs were rescued from various pounds around New Zealand. Thanks to these pounds for assisting; Whanganui Pound, Far North District Pound, Henderson Pound, Ruapeha District Pound
